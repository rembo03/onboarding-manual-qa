User Manual for Me – Badal Moreya

 Overview  
This “User Manual” explains how I work, communicate, and collaborate best.  
My goal is to make teamwork smoother by being clear about my preferences, strengths, and quirks.

---

How I Work Best  
- I do well when tasks and expectations are clear.  
- I like having written instructions so I can refer back to them.  
- I prefer understanding the bigger picture before starting detailed work.  
- I stay productive when I have a structured plan or checklist.  
- I like deep-focus sessions where I can work without interruptions.

---

How I Prefer to Communicate  
- I’m comfortable with async messages (Slack/Discord/GitHub comments).  
- I prefer direct, simple communication without too much formality.  
- If something is urgent, a quick message or call is okay.  
- I appreciate when teammates are clear and specific in their requests.

---

 What I Need From Teammates  
- Clear instructions or examples when assigning tasks.  
- Honest but respectful feedback.  
- Quick clarification when something is blocking my work.  
- Flexibility when learning new tools or processes.  
- Patience if I need time to understand a new workflow.

---

 What Helps Me Stay Focused & Productive  
- A quiet workspace with minimal distractions.  
- Breaking work into smaller steps.  
- Using to-do lists and structured plans.  
- Taking short breaks to reset, especially when doing detailed testing.  
- Simple music or white noise helps me stay in flow.


 How I Give & Receive Feedback  
**Giving feedback:**  
- I try to be direct and clear without sounding harsh.  
- I focus on the issue, not the person.

  Receiving feedback 
- I prefer direct, actionable feedback (e.g., “Change X to Y”).  
- I appreciate when someone explains the reason behind the feedback.  
- Written feedback works best so I can review it calmly.

---

Things to Know About My Work Style  
- I like to ask clarifying questions early instead of guessing.  
- I may take a little time to process new information before responding.  
- I prefer written instructions over long meetings.  
- I stay reliable and consistent once expectations are clear.  
- I enjoy collaborating but also appreciate independent work time.  
- I try to keep communication simple and avoid confusion.

---

How to Work Best With Me  
- Be clear and honest.  
- Tell me the priority level of tasks so I can plan properly.  
- If something is urgent, highlight it clearly.  
- If I miss something, I appreciate a direct reminder.  
- Share feedback openly — I use it to improve.

---

My Commitments to the Team  
- I will communicate clearly and respectfully.  
- I will share updates proactively (especially blockers).  
- I will be reliable with deadlines.  
- I will maintain professionalism and positive teamwork.  
- I will keep learning and improving my QA skills.

Task Completion
